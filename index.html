Meet the cast

The Roommates.

Noobie
⚡ Full Energy Mode

When the energy hits, nothing can stop Noobie. Loud, enthusiastic, and always has seventeen plans at once. Decorates his room in orange paint and coding posters, wears a grey hat with a red R, and works so hard he falls asleep on the sofa. Somehow always makes it work.

G
😐 Low Battery Mode

G is just trying to exist. Calm, deadpan, and perpetually tired. Gets things done at his own pace — food, sleep, repeat. Has to share an apartment with Noobie, which is... a lot. His name starts with G and even he doesn't know why.

Recurring cast

Dex
🛒 The Cashier

The cashier at the local shop. Noobie and G keep running into him. There's more to Dex than just scanning items — he shows up more than you'd expect.

The show

What's it about?

New apartment. New chaos.

The Noob Show follows Noobie and G as they settle into their new life as roommates at apartment 317. One charges in headfirst. One just wants a nap. Together they navigate the everyday chaos of adult life — the stuff nobody teaches you — and somehow figure it out one episode at a time.

The show isn't aimed at kids, but if they watch it the worst that'll happen is they become a bit like G. An original hand-animated series by noobie, part of the DEEB family. Coming to YouTube.
